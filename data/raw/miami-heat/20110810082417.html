1990 Jazz-HEAT Retro Diary

Photo Credit: NBAE
The night before Christmas Eve in 1990, the Miami HEAT were on a ten-game losing streak with the Utah Jazz in town. But despite their youth and lack of polish, Miami had already beaten Utah at Miami Arena twice in its first two years. So, against Karl Malone and John Stockton, only one streak could continue.
